About Us

Asia Euro University (AEU) is a leading educational institution committed to providing high-quality education and research opportunities. Founded in 2003, we have been dedicated to academic excellence and innovation.

Five-year strategic plan

To become a university with excellence, prestige, and productivity in human resource training and research to contribute to the government in studies and Administrative work, which is a service, is aimed at increasing human resource capacity to serve in the job market both domestically and internationally.

Vision

Strengthen the role of training and research in the fields of science and technology to ensure Cambodia's economic development and future prosperity.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that prepares students for leadership and service in a diverse global society.

Values

Promote the technology sector with quality and respect for professionalism.

Goal

Standing on the principles of training and management of the university based on technology, it ensures modernization of curricula, physical materials in accordance with standards, increases cooperation, and promotes research.
